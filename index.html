User Guide

Gameplay Elements

Possess Objects

  • Make use of their unique properties to get around obstacles!

Solve Puzzles with Increasing Difficulty

  • Experiment and come up with interesting solutions!

Controls (in game)

  • Use the mouse to rotate the camera
  • Use WASD keys to move
  • E to possess/un-possess objects
  • Q to bring up game info window
  • ESC for more options
